## mode switch
- `<ESC>`: switch to **normal mode**
- normal mode `<i>`: switch to **insert mode**
- normal mode `<v>`: switch to **visual mode**
### Quit Vim
- `:wq`: save and quit
- `:q!`: quit without saving
- `:w`: save without quitting
